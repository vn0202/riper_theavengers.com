The Avengers

The Avengers is a powerhouse jazz-fusion band uniting four world-class musicians: Beledo (guitar), Adam Holzman (keyboards), Lincoln Goines (bass), and Kim Plainfield (drums). Blending the intensity of rock, the complexity of jazz, and the flavor of Latin rhythms, The Avengers push the boundaries of modern instrumental music. Known for their electrifying performances and sophisticated compositions, the band has captivated audiences across South America, Europe, and beyond.
